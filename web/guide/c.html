Qibla Direction
📖 Qur'an Al-Baqarah 2:144
فَوَلِّ وَجْهَكَ شَطْرَ الْمَسْجِدِ الْحَرَامِ ۚ وَحَيْثُ مَا كُنتُمْ فَوَلُّوا وُجُوهَكُمْ شَطْرَهُ
"So turn your face toward al-Masjid al-Haram. And wherever you [believers] are, turn your faces toward it [in prayer]."
This verse established the Kaaba in Mecca as the eternal Qibla for Muslims across the world.
📜 Hadith Bukhari & Muslim
إِذَا قُمْتَ إِلَى الصَّلَاةِ فَأَسْبِغِ الْوُضُوءَ، ثُمَّ اسْتَقْبِلِ الْقِبْلَةَ فَكَبِّرْ
"When you stand up for prayer, perform Wudu perfectly, then face the Qibla and say: Allahu Akbar."
Facing the Qibla is a fundamental condition for the validity of the obligatory prayers.
