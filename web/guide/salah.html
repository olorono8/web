You've Walked Through Salah
رَبَّنَا تَقَبَّلْ مِنَّا إِنَّكَ أَنْتَ السَّمِيعُ الْعَلِيمُ
"Our Lord, accept this from us. You are the All-Hearing, the All-Knowing."
This is the same dua used to close an act of worship — fitting to end this guide with it too.

Five prayers, spread through every kind of day — dawn, midday, afternoon, sunset, night — regardless of what the day held. Go slowly at first; understanding grows with repetition.
